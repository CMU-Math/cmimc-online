How to Register

For Students

  1. Create an account on this website. Make sure to select "Contestant" as your role.
  2. If you want to join an existing team, ask someone on that team to send you the invite link
  3. If you wan to create a new team, go to {{ request.scheme }}://{{ request.get_host }}{% url 'contest_list' %} and select the contest you want to register for.
  4. Choose a team name
  5. Invite other members to your team with the given invite link.
  6. Once all your team members are finalized, click the Register button on your team's page

For Coaches

  1. Create an account on this website. Make sure to select "Coach" as your role.
  2. Go to {{ request.scheme }}://{{ request.get_host }}{% url 'contest_list' %} and select the contest you want to register for.
  3. Choose a team name
  4. Invite students to your team with the given invite link.
  5. Once all your team members are finalized, click Register
  6. Repeat for each team you want to make
