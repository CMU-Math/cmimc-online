How to Register

All students will need an account on this website, which they use to submit their solutions on the day of the contest. Only one person (either a student or coach) needs to create the team.

For Students

  1. Create an account on this website. Make sure to select "Contestant" as your role.
  2. Go to {{ request.scheme }}://{{ request.get_host }}{% url 'contest_list' %} and select the contest you want to register for.
  3. Choose a team name.
  4. Invite other members to your team with the given invite link.
  5. Once all your team members are finalized, click the Register button on your team's page
  6. Make sure each team member fills out the waiver and submits it via this form by January 16th.
  7. If you're interested in a CMIMC Programming T-shirt, fill out this form by January 3rd.

For Coaches

  1. Create an account on this website. Make sure to select "Coach" as your role.
  2. Go to {{ request.scheme }}://{{ request.get_host }}{% url 'contest_list' %} and select the contest you want to register for.
  3. Choose a team name
  4. Invite students to your team with the given invite link.
  5. Once all your team members are finalized, click Register.
  6. Repeat this process for each team you want to create.
  7. Make sure each student fills out the waiver and submits it via this form by January 16th.
  8. If you or your students are interested in a CMIMC Programming T-shirt, fill out this form by January 3rd.
