Math and Programming Contests

Welcome!

The Carnegie Mellon Informatics and Mathematics Competition (CMIMC) is an annual math and computer science competition held at Carnegie Mellon University by CMU students. This year's contest will be held on January 30th, 2021.

Announcements

  • The CMIMC 2021 Math Contest will be held entirely online due to COVID-19. See the registration page for instructions to register.
  • CMIMC is starting a brand-new programming contest this year! It's run by CMU students, the same team behind the CMIMC math competition. This is a team contest, with up to 3 people per team. Make sure you register a team by January 16th, 2021! See the registration page for instructions to register. Due to COVID-19, it will be entirely online. The contest consists of an AI Round and an Optimization Round. See the info page for more details.
