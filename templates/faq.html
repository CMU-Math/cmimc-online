General FAQ

How can we request a waiver for the registration fee?
Email us at cmimc@math.cmu.edu and let us know about your circumstances. We'll do our best to accommodate!
Can I buy a CMIMC shirt?
CMIMC t-shirts come for free with the registration fee for each competition. However, they are also sold at the competition for $__ each.
Can I come to CMIMC as an individual?
Yes, you can! But a full team consists of 4-6 people, so you will participate as an incompelete team.
Who can participate?
Participants must be high school students.
How many people per team?
For both the programming and math competitions, teams consist of 4-6 people, but incomplete teams (including teams of 1) will be accommodated on the contest date. Teams are not required to be in geographical proximity.
Who runs the CMIMC?
CMIMC is run by Carnegie Mellon undergraduate students.
What types of mini-events will there be?
Mini-events may include ... (a talk by a guest speaker, a panel of current Carnegie Mellon students, and small competitions like Rubik's cube or chess).
What awards will be offered?
Top teams will receive trophies, and top individuals will receive medals.
What do I do if I do not understand a problem during the test?
You should notify your testing proctor over Discord, who will relay your question to the CMIMC staff.
What do I do if I disagree with an answer or solution to a problem after the test?
You should fill out the Google form for problem disputes.
What if I am caught cheating?
Such situations will be dealt with on a case-by-case basis. At the very least, the cheating individual will be disqualified, and the team will have to compete with the remaining individuals.
What should I do if I think someone cheated?
You should contact any CMIMC staff, who will notify the competition directors.
Do good results at the CMIMC competition contribute to my college application to Carnegie Mellon University?
CMIMC is not affiliated with the Carnegie Mellon admissions office; however, you should include it in your application if you competed in the CMIMC to demonstrate your motivation and excitment for math and computer science.

Programming Competition FAQ

Do we register individually or as a team?
You will register as a team. Each competitor will be required to provide an email address so we can add them to the private competition Discord. One person will also have to specify an address for their team and all trophies and swag will be sent to this address.
How will the competition be proctored?
The competition will be proctored via Discord. Competitors will be added to a channel and will have a designated proctor who will answer their questions as they come up.

Math Competition FAQ

What is the difference between the two divisions of the math competition?
The only difference is that the individual rounds of different divisions will consists of different problems. The rounds are weighted differently when individual scores in calculating the team aggregate score.
Am I required to have my video on during each round?
Yes! During the Individual Rounds for CMIMC Math, your video must show your hands and work area. If possible, try to include your face as well, but your hands must always be in the frame.
Do we still need one chaperone per team?
Yes, you will need to specify an adult responsible for your team.
